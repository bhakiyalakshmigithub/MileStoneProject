OUR PRODUCTS

Chicken Chettinad

Originating in the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu.It is made by cooking marinated chicken with a special paste which gives the recipe its flavour.

Thalassery Biriyani

Originating from a place called Thalassery in Kerala .Instead of using normal basmati rice, It is used to make this delicacy giving the dish its distinct flavour.

Pandi Curry

This delicious recipe comes from the region of Coorg in Karnataka. Hence there is a multiplicity of delicious non vegetarian dishes like Pandi curry.

The most famous biryani cuisine of India- the Hyderabadi dum Biryani is a dish made with aromatic basmati rice with dahi marinated chicken.

Masala Dosa
